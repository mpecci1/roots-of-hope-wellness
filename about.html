Meet Elyse Fearon

A nurse practitioner who left symptom-chasing behind to practice whole-person care — and walk with you toward hope.

roots Her story

Looking deeper than the symptom

Elyse spent years caring for patients in hospitals and outpatient offices. Again and again she watched conventional medicine name a symptom, hand over a prescription, and move on — without ever asking why someone felt unwell in the first place.

She knew there was a better way. So she trained in functional medicine and built a practice around the whole person — the body, the mind, and the spirit — and the belief that there is hope to be well again.

Nurse Practitioner Registered Nurse IFM Certified Health & Wellness Coach
“

True wellness is cultivated when your body, mind and spirit work together in harmony.

— Elyse Fearon

What I believe

Care built on three convictions

These shape every plan, every visit, and every conversation we’ll have together.

Start your journey
  • Treat the root, not the leaf.

    Symptoms are signals. We trace them back to the systems underneath — gut, hormones, stress, nutrition — and tend the soil they’re growing in.

  • Honor the whole person.

    You are body, mind and spirit — and they heal together. Your story, your stress, and your faith all belong in the room.

  • Hold on to hope.

    No matter how long you’ve struggled, there is hope to be well again. My job is to help you find your light and walk with you toward it.

faith Faith woven in

For those who want it, faith and spirituality are part of the healing here. We believe spiritual connection is fundamental to recovery — and that you were made to live well in body, mind and spirit.
