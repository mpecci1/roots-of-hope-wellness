Good questions

Before you book

What is functional medicine, really?
It’s a root-cause approach to health. Instead of treating each symptom on its own, we look at how your whole system — nutrition, gut, hormones, stress, sleep — works together, and address what’s actually driving how you feel.
Are visits virtual?
Yes — all visits are virtual, so you can meet with Elyse from the comfort of home, wherever you are. All you need is a quiet spot and an internet connection.
Do you take insurance?
No — I don’t take insurance directly. But I can give you a detailed receipt (called a superbill) that you can submit to your own insurance company for possible reimbursement, if your plan offers out-of-network benefits.
How soon will I feel better?
Everyone is different. Many people notice early shifts within a few weeks, while deeper healing unfolds over the months we work together. We set realistic milestones and adjust as your body responds.
Is this faith-based — what if I’m not religious?
Faith is woven in for those who want it, and the medicine is for everyone. You’ll always be met with respect and excellent, evidence-informed care — no matter where you are on your spiritual journey.
